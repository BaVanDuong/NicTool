Functions for Zone Records

  • delete_zonerecord Delete a Zone Record.

  • edit_zone_record Modify the details of an existing Zone Record.

  • get_zone_record Get the details for a specific Zone Record.

  • get_zone_record_log Get a list of log entries for actions performed on Zone records in a certain Zone.

  • get_zone_record_log_entry Get a specific log entry from the Zone Record log.

  • get_zone_records Get the Zone records in a specific Zone.

  • new_zone_record Create a new Zone Record.
