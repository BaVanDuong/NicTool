Functions for Delegation

  • delegate_zones Delegate a list of Zones to a Group with a set of permissions.

  • delegate_zone_records Delegate a list of Zone Records to a Group with a set of permissions.

  • delete_zone_delegation Remove the delegation of a Zone to a Group.

  • delete_zone_record_delegation Remove the delegation of a Zone Record to a Group.

  • edit_zone_delegation Modify the permissions for a delegation of a Zone to a Group.

  • edit_zone_record_delegation Modify the permissions for a delegation of a Zone Record to a Group.

  • get_delegated_zones Get the list of Zones that have been delegated to a Group.

  • get_delegated_zone_records Get the list of Zones Records that have been delegated to a Group.

  • get_zone_delegates Get the list of Groups that have been made delegates of a Zone.

  • get_zone_record_delegates Get the list of Groups that have been made delegates of a Zone Record.
